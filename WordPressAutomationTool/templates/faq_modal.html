General Questions

What is WordPress Automation Tool?

WordPress Automation Tool is a platform that helps you automatically set up and configure WordPress websites with customized themes and content.

How does it work?

Our tool connects to your hosting account, installs WordPress, configures themes, and generates content based on your preferences.
