10 - Stage Timeline Guide

Stage 1

Getting Started - Connect with server using cPanel login credentials. You may need cPanel or Softaculous API keys as optional requirements for automatic WP installation.

Stage 2

Setting Up Your WP - Create Wordpress database Credentials and Configurations using your own UN & PW.

Stage 3

Category Selection - Once installed the Wordpress software, select your desired website category.

Stage 4

Theme Selection - Choosing your preferred theme template and associated plugins to be installed.

Stage 5

Menu & Content Generation - AI will provide you a list of menu to Auto-populate according to your website category or you may Continue to Content Generation stage.

Stage 6

Premium Plans - Multiple Menu & Contents Generation are possible by monthly subsciption models. Payment made by Secured Stripe Gateway.

Stage 7

AI Menu Generation - Automatic Top Menu creation according to your website category selection.

Stage 8

AI Content Generation - Automatic multiple contents creation according to your website category & Subsciption selection.

Stage 9

Performance Tuning - Application of SEO optimizations, Google Analytics and other speedup processes.

Stage 10

Launch & Maintenance - Going live and managing updates.
