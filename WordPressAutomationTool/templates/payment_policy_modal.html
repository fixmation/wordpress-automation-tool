Payment Policy

At WordPress Automation Tool, we are committed to providing transparent and secure payment processes.

1. Subscription Plans

We offer various subscription plans to suit different needs:

  • Free Plan: Basic features with limited access
  • Basic Plan: Essential tools for beginners
  • Professional Plan: Advanced features for professionals
  • Premium Plan: Complete automation for businesses

2. Payment Methods & Fees

We accept major credit cards through our secure Stripe payment gateway.

Additional charges may apply:

  • Stripe processing fee: 2.9% + $0.30 per successful charge
  • International card fee: Additional 1% for non-local cards
  • Currency conversion fee: Up to 2% when paying in non-USD currencies
  • Bank/card issuer fees: May vary by provider

3. Billing

Subscriptions are billed monthly and automatically renewed unless canceled.

All fees are charged in USD and converted to your local currency at current exchange rates.

4. Refund Policy

We offer prorated refunds for unused portions of your subscription.

Processing fees are non-refundable.
